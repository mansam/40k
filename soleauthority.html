Destination:
Beacon of Nyx
Nyx-Prime and attendant Forge Worlds
All Imperial Authorities

By right of arms and under the eternal mandate of the Emperor, know this: The Beacon of Nyx and the surrounding forges are henceforth placed under the direct custody of the Dark Angels Chapter. This action is necessitated by corruption and dereliction among the Mechanicus elements entrusted with their charge, whose failures have invited the storms that now imperil the Reach. Civil authority and Mechanicus oversight are suspended. Administratum edicts and lesser writs are annulled until such time as order is restored. The output of the forges remains dedicated to the service of the Imperium. Its distribution will be overseen solely by the Chapter. No petition shall be heard save through the appointed officers of the Chapter. No vessel shall pass into or out of the Beacon without warrant issued under our seal. This proclamation is final. Compliance is obedience; resistance is treachery.
